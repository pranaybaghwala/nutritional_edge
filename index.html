About Shivika Gandhi

Nutrition that goes beyond the plate

Hi, I'm Shivika Gandhi — a registered dietitian and M.Sc Food & Nutrition graduate with a passion for helping people build a healthier relationship with food. I believe that sustainable health isn't about restricting what you love, but about understanding how nourishment works for your unique body.

Through science-backed, culturally sensitive meal planning and one-on-one counselling, I've helped 500+ clients across weight management, diabetes, PCOD, thyroid disorders, gut health, and sports nutrition — all without crash diets.

  • 📍 Based in Gurugram, available online across India
